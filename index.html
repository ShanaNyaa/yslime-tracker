Available Gyroscope Sensors:

We offer several options for gyroscope sensors to fit your budget and performance needs.

  • LSM6DSO: A balanced option for cost and performance.
  • LSM6DSV: High-performance tracking for advanced users.
  • BNO085: Industry-standard, premium reliability and stability, used in production SlimeVR.
